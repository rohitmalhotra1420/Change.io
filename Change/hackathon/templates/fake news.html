History and concept

Blockchain have proven succesfull for bitcoins over these years . Every transaction of the bitcoin gets recorded on the Blockchain and thus can be tracked down to the last source with a lot of ease.A block chain is a transaction database shared by all nodes participating in a system based on the Bitcoin protocol. A full copy of a currency's block chain contains every transaction ever executed in the currency. With this information, one can find out how much value belonged to each address at any point in history.
